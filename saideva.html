THE HISTORY


Shirdi is a tiny hamlet in the district of AhmednagAar of Maharastra . Many great monks and saints made Shirdi as their dwelling place and practised the Sanatana dharma. One among the is Lord Sai baba . He firstly appeared as a 16 year old child in shirdi .He meditated over there for few years over there and disappeared from shirdi . Again after few years he appeared in Shirdi with the marraige troup of Chand Patel as a fakir . By observing the arrival of that young fakir the priest of Khandobha temple shri Mahalsapathi invited him into village as "AUO SAI" which means wlcome SAI the after that young fakir was named after "SAI BABA" . Baba had choosen a ruines mosque as his dwelling place and he named it after "DWARAKAMAI" . He lived in DWARAKAMAI until his last breath . He litted a holy fire camp and named it after "DHUNI" . The ashes of dhuni is distributed as UDI PRASADAM . This udi acts as a best medicine for all kinds of pandemics . Many miracles were shown by baba in dwarakamai . He left his last breath on 15 October 1918 on Vijayadashami day . His holy death body is burried in Bhuti vaada whichis now popularly known as Samadhi Mandir . Due to the presence of Sai baba and his invisable personality in shirdi this village stands for many miracles .
