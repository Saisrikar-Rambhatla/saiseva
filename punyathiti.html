Punyathiti

Punyathiti is the one of the major festivals of Shirdi . This is celebrated for three days in shirdi .
The very bigining day kakad aarti is performed as usual and after completion of kakad aarti poti procession takes place . In dwarakamai Sai saccharitra is read for the whole day . Due to pothi readings Dwarakamai remains open for whole day . At 9:15 pm palkhi procession takes place on this day . After returning of pallkhi Shej aarti is performed .
The second day or Punyatithi day after completion of Kakad aarti pothi is returned to samadhi mandir and a samll aarti is performed in samadhi mandir and then Mangal snan is performed . Madhyan aarti will be performed at 12:30P.M on this day. For the convinience of devotees the samadhi mandir remains open on this day . Also procession of charriot takes place at 9:00P.M in the streets of shirdi
The last day or on conclusion day Rudrabhishek takes place in gurusthan mandir . At 12:00Noon Dahi handi or Gopal kala will be performed in Samadhi mandir . With these rituals Punyathiti utsav willbe endedup. This utsav is performed in mid october generally according to Indian lunar calender this falls on Ashiwiyuja Shudda navami to Ashwiyuya shudda ekadashi.
