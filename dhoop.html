Dhoop Aarti

The ritual in samadhi mandir that takes place during sunset time is DHOOP AARTI . The preists of temple sings 10 well knowned marathi song im this aarti .
This aarti is followed by paadyapooja to holyfeet of shri Sai baba . At the end of aarti holy food is offered to shri Sai baba . Then devotees are allowed to seek baba's blessings . This does'nt have a fixed timming as per the sunset this ritual is performed and this lasts upto 30 minutes .
