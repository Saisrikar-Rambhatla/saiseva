MADHYAN AARTI

The major ritual that takes place at 12:00 noon is MADHYAN AARTI . This ritual begins with offering food or nivedhya to Sai baba . Then it is followed by an aarti which consists of 10 popurlar marathi songs .
It is belived that baba accepts the offered food and blesses all his devotees all over the world by listening to this aarti . This lasts upto 12:30P.M.
