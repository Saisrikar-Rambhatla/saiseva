MANGAL SNAN

Mangal sanan is the very next ritual performed in the Samadhi madir.After completion of Kakad aarti the moquito net covered of shri Sai baba is opened and the sacred cloth wounded over Sai baba's holy idol is removed and the bare idol of is shown to the devotees . The holy songs and bhjans are sung from 5:00A.M to 5:05A.M.
At 5:05 A.M the holy bath is performed to Sai baba . Parlelly vedic hyms are sung by the priests of temple . This ritual lasts upto 5 minutes .
