Alankaram and Laghu Aarti

The very next ritual performed to Sai baba is alakaram . In this ritual Sai baba's idol is rinsed and the idol is adorned with a beautiful silk cloth . Along with silk cloth the idol is adorned with jweles and floweres . Parlelly vishnu sahasranaama and marathi bhajans ass sung . This contiues upto 5:30A.M .
After adorning beautiful idol of shri Sai baba idol a samll aarti is performed to Saibaba . This is also known as LAGHU AARTI . In this ritual food or nivedya is offered to Saibaba and a small marathi song named SHIRDI MAJHE PANDHARPURA is sung by the priests . This ritual ends up with blowing shankam . This takes place at 5:35A.M and lasts upto 5 minutes.
