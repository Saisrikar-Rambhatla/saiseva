Shej Aarti

The final ritual that takes place in samadhi mandir is SHEJ AARTI . Before performing this aarti a golden glass of water and holy food is offered to sai baba . During this aarti baba's idol is adorned with a cotton cloth and Rudraksha maala . Then a popular 10 marathi songs are sung by the priests in temple. After completion of aarti the idol of sai baba and the holy samadhi of sai baba are enclosed within mosquito net . This aarti begins at 10:30P.M and lasts upto 10:50P.M . The doors of temple remains shut from 11:00P.M to 4:00A.M.
